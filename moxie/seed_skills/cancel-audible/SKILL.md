# Cancel Audible (UK)

Audible's cancel link is real but deliberately buried under Account details,
then guarded by a multi-screen retention flow — decline everything to get
through to the actual cancellation.

## Steps

1. Sign in with your Amazon account; go to your name (top nav) → Account details.
2. Find "Cancel membership" — it's a small link, easy to miss.
3. Audible will push several retention screens in sequence: pause membership
   (up to 3 months), a discounted plan, and free credits. Click "No thanks,
   continue cancelling" (desktop) or "Continue cancelling" / "Cancel anyway"
   (mobile web) on each one.
4. Pick a cancellation reason from the dropdown and confirm.
5. Keep the confirmation email — your membership stays active until the end
   of the current billing period.

## Notes

- Unused credits are forfeited when the final billing cycle ends — spend them
  first if you can. Audiobooks already bought with a credit or cash stay in
  your library permanently, even after cancelling.
- Billed via Apple or Google instead of Audible directly? Cancel there (Apple:
  Settings → Subscriptions; Google: play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions)
  — those credits don't expire either.
- Not ready to fully leave? Pausing (once per 12 months) keeps your credits
  instead of forfeiting them — a real alternative worth surfacing before
  outright cancelling.
- Cancellation isn't possible from the mobile app itself — it has to go
  through a browser (mobile web or desktop).
