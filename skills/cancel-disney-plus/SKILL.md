# Cancel Disney+

Cancel from the account's subscription page; access runs to the end of the
billing period. Rotating Disney+ on/off around the shows you actually watch
is the classic money-saver.

## Steps

1. Sign in and open the subscription section.
2. Choose your plan, then "Cancel subscription".
3. Ignore the pause/downgrade retention screens unless one is genuinely better.
4. Keep the confirmation email.

## Notes

- Subscribed via an app store or a telco bundle (e.g. O2)? Cancel there instead.
