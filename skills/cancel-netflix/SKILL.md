# Cancel Netflix

Netflix's cancel page is one click once you're signed in — no retention maze.
Plan stays active until the end of the billing period, and they keep your
profile for 10 months in case you come back (rotating is genuinely easy).

## Steps

1. Sign in with your usual account.
2. Confirm on the "Finish cancellation" button.
3. Screenshot the confirmation page for your records.

## Notes

- Billed via an app store instead? Cancel it there (Apple: Settings → Subscriptions;
  Google: play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions) — the website can't.
- Check the paired charge in your statement stops next cycle; if it doesn't,
  dispute with the receipt attached.
