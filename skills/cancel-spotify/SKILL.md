# Cancel Spotify Premium

Spotify cancels from the account page; you keep Premium until the period ends,
then drop to the free tier (your playlists survive).

## Steps

1. Sign in and open the subscription page.
2. Scroll to your plan and choose "Cancel Premium".
3. Decline the discount retention offer (or take it, if that's the win you wanted).
4. Keep the confirmation email.

## Notes

- Billed through iTunes or your mobile carrier? Cancel there instead.
- Student plan? It just lapses if you don't re-verify — no action needed.
