# Cancel PureGym (UK)

No contract on standard plans, but cancellation is via the members' area AND
your bank: PureGym collects by Direct Debit, so cancelling the mandate is the
backstop that actually stops the money.

## Steps

1. Log in to the members' area and go to Membership → Cancel.
2. Complete their form; note the final payment date they quote.
3. In your banking app, cancel the PureGym Direct Debit AFTER that final
   payment — that makes the cancellation stick.
4. Keep the confirmation email/screenshot.

## Notes

- Cancelling only the Direct Debit (without step 1-2) can leave an "unpaid"
  flag with them; do both.
- Fixed-term deals (rare) need the term served or a fee — check the quoted date.
