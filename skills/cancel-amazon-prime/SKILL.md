# Cancel Amazon Prime (UK)

Amazon's flow has several "are you sure" screens — that's the dark pattern,
keep clicking. UK consumer law means unused periods are refundable.

## Steps

1. Sign in; the link goes straight into the cancellation pipeline.
2. Click "End membership" through EVERY confirmation screen (there are 3-4;
   anything else — "Keep my benefits", "Remind me later" — keeps you paying).
3. If you haven't used Prime since the last renewal, pick the refund option.
4. Keep the confirmation email.

## Notes

- Prime Video-only subs cancel at primevideo.com → Account → Channels.
- If a renewal just hit and you've not used it, chat support refunds it —
  cite the unused period.
